Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to enter credit card information for the free plan?

No, the free plan requires no payment information. Just sign up and start using it.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When downgrading, you'll need to ensure your library fits within the new plan limits.

What happens if I exceed my song limit?

You won't be able to upload additional songs until you either upgrade your plan or remove songs to get under your plan limit.
